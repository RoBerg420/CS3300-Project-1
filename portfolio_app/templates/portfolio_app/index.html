Geological Formations around Colorado!!


What are outcrops?

Outcrops could usually consists of multiple formations.
Formations themselves are comprised of many different type of rocks from different time periods.


What is a formation?

A geological formation, or simply formation, is a body of rock having a consistent set of physical characteristics that distinguishes it from adjacent bodies of rock, and which occupies a particular position in the layers of rock exposed in a geographical region


What defines a rock?

Rocks are made of minerals wich in turn are made of elements.
If you don't know what defines a mineral just remember D.I.S.C.O!

D.I.S.C.O being an acronym of course!

  • Defined Crystalline Structure
  • Inorganic
  • Solid
  • Chemical Composition
  • Occurs Naturally
